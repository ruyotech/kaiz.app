# The Agile Life System

## What is Agile?

Agile is the methodology that transformed how the world's best companies build products. Companies like Apple, Google, Spotify, and Amazon use Agile to ship world-class software every single week.

**The core principles:**
- Work in short, focused cycles (Sprints)
- Commit only to what you can actually deliver
- Measure everything
- Improve continuously
- Adapt quickly when things change

## Why Agile for Life?

Your life is the most important product you'll ever build. Yet most people "manage" it with:
- Endless to-do lists that never end
- Vague annual goals that fade by February
- Reactive firefighting instead of proactive planning
- No measurement of actual progress
- No systematic improvement

**Kaiz brings Agile to personal life:**

| Agile Concept | In Software | In Kaiz (Your Life) |
|---------------|-------------|---------------------|
| **Sprint** | 2-week development cycle | 1-week life cycle |
| **Backlog** | Features to build | Goals & tasks to accomplish |
| **Story Points** | Effort estimation | Task complexity (1-21 scale) |
| **Velocity** | Team capacity | Your personal capacity |
| **Standup** | Daily team sync | Daily personal check-in |
| **Retrospective** | What went well/wrong | Weekly reflection |
| **Epic** | Large feature | Major life goal |
| **Product Owner** | Decides priorities | You |
| **Scrum Master** | Coaches the team | SensAI (your AI coach) |

## The Weekly Sprint Cycle

Every week in Kaiz follows a proven rhythm:

### Sunday: Sprint Planning
- Review your backlog
- Select tasks for the week
- Commit within your velocity
- Set your sprint goal

### Monday-Friday: Daily Execution
- Morning standup (2 minutes)
- Focus on committed tasks
- Track progress in real-time
- Handle blockers immediately

### Saturday: Sprint Review
- What did you ship?
- What's your completion rate?
- Celebrate wins

### Sunday: Retrospective
- What went well?
- What didn't?
- One improvement for next sprint

**This cycle repeats 52 times per year.** 52 opportunities to improve. 52 chances to ship your life.

# Calendar Sync & Integration

## Why Calendar Sync Matters

Your time is finite. Your external calendar (work meetings, appointments, events) blocks real hours. Kaiz needs to see this to plan accurately.

**Without sync:** You commit 40 points but have 20 hours of meetings. You fail.

**With sync:** Kaiz sees your meetings, calculates available time, adjusts recommended capacity. You succeed.

## Supported Calendars

### Google Calendar
- Full two-way sync
- Multiple calendar support
- Event details imported
- Real-time updates
- Color coding preserved

### Apple Calendar (iCloud)
- Full two-way sync
- All iOS calendars
- Event details imported
- Push notifications
- Seamless on iPhone/iPad

### Outlook Calendar
- Read-only sync
- Work calendar support
- Meeting detection
- Time block recognition

## How Sync Works

### Reading External Events
Kaiz imports events from your connected calendars:
- Meeting title (or "Busy" for private)
- Start and end time
- Location (if available)
- All-day event flag

These appear on your Kaiz calendar as **blocked time**.

### Capacity Calculation

Kaiz automatically adjusts your capacity:

```
Base Weekly Hours: 40 hours
- Synced meetings: 15 hours
- Sleep (auto-blocked): 56 hours (8h √ó 7 days)
= Available Hours: 25 hours

Your velocity: 30 points
Capacity ratio: 25/40 = 62.5%
Adjusted capacity: 30 √ó 0.625 = ~19 points recommended
```

SensAI uses this to warn about overcommitment.

### Creating Events in Kaiz
When you create time-blocked tasks:
- Optionally sync to external calendar
- Appears in Google/Apple/Outlook
- Two-way: edit in either place
- Delete syncs too

## Calendar Views

### Day Schedule View
- 24-hour timeline
- External events shown in gray
- Kaiz tasks shown in color
- Available slots visible
- Tap to add task to time slot

### Week Overview
- All 7 days visible
- Event density indicator
- Busy days highlighted
- Help plan sprint capacity

## Setup Process

1. Go to Settings ‚Üí Integrations
2. Select calendar provider
3. Authenticate with Google/Apple/Microsoft
4. Choose calendars to sync
5. Set sync frequency
6. Done ‚Äî events appear immediately

## Privacy

- Kaiz only reads event times and titles
- No access to email or contacts
- You control which calendars sync
- Revoke access anytime
- Data never sold or shared
