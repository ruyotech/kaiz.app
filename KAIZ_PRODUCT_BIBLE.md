# 1. THE PHILOSOPHY: Why Agile for Humanity?

**The Problem:**
Modern life is a complex engineering project managed with stone-age tools. We juggle careers, health, finances, and relationships using fragmented to-do lists, sticky notes, and anxiety. We set New Year's resolutions (Waterfalls) and fail by February because feedback loops are too long.

** The Solution:**
**Kaiz** (derived from *Kaizen* - Continuous Improvement) is not a task manager. It is an **Agile Life Operating System**.

It takes the methodology that built the modern world—**Agile Software Development**—and applies it to the most important product you will ever ship: **Your Potential.**

Kaiz replaces:
*   *Vague Resolutions* with **2-Week Sprints**.
*   *Busy Work* with **Impact Velocity**.
*   *Burnout* with **Data-Driven Capacity Planning**.
*   *Chaotic Thoughts* with **Structured Backlogs**.

We believe that winners and losers have the same goals. The difference is **systems**. Kaiz is that system.

# 2. THE CORE ENGINE: Sprint-Based Living

Life at Kaiz is not measured in days or years. It is measured in **Sprints**.

### The Sprint Cycle (2 Weeks)
Just like a high-performance tech team, you operate in focused bursts.
1.  **Planning (Sunday Evening):**
    *   Review the **Backlog**.
    *   Select **Epics** (Launch Business, Run Marathon).
    *   Estimate effort using **Story Points** (Fibonacci Sequence: 1, 2, 3, 5, 8).
    *   **Commit** to a Sprint Goal (e.g., "Ship MVP" or "Deep Reconnection").
    *   *System Check:* If you commit to 60 points but your historical velocity is 40, SensAI triggers a **Capacity Alert**.

2.  **Execution (Mon-Fri):**
    *   **Focus Mode:** Work on COMMITTED tasks.
    *   **The "Carried Over" Badge of Shame:** Tasks missed in Sprint A automatically roll to Sprint B with a prominent orange badge. This visualizes procrastination debt.
    *   **Eisenhower Categorization:** All tasks are auto-sorted:
        *   *Q1: Urgent/Important* (Do Now)
        *   *Q2: Not Urgent/Important* (Schedule - Where growth happens)
        *   *Q3: Urgent/Not Important* (Delegate)
        *   *Q4: Not Urgent/Not Important* (Delete)

3.  **Review & Retrospective (Friday Afternoon):**
    *   **Velocity Chart:** Visual bar graph showing Points Committed vs. Points Completed.
    *   **The Delta:** "You improved your velocity by 12% this sprint."
    *   **Retrospective Questions:** "What went well? What blocked you?"

### The Velocity Metric
We do not track "Hours Modelled." We track **Impact Points**.
*   **High Velocity:** You are shipping outcomes.
*   **Low Velocity:** You are stuck in "busy work" or blockers.
*   **Burnout Protection:** The system learns your max capacity. If you try to overload yourself, it stops you.

# 3. SENSAI: The Cybernetic Scrum Master

**SensAI is NOT a Chatbot.**
Most AI assistants are "Passive Search Engines." SensAI is an **Active Project Manager.**

### The Personality Profile
SensAI is engineered to be:
*   **Ruthless:** It ignores small talk. *"ABSOLUTE RULE #0: NEVER CREATE NOTE FOR GREETINGS."*
*   **Proactive:** It doesn't wait for you to ask. It interrupts you when you are off track.
*   **Structured:** It thinks in Entities (Tasks, Events, Epics), not conversation.

### The Daily Standup
Every morning, SensAI initiates a structured briefing:
1.  **Yesterday:** *"You completed 12 points. You missed the 'Gym' task."*
2.  **Today:** *"You have 3 critical tasks. The big rock is 'Board Presentation'."*
3.  **Blockers:** *"You have back-to-back meetings from 2 PM. When will you do deep work?"*

### The Intervention Engine
SensAI monitors your **Life Telemetry**.
*   *Scenario:* You've scheduled 4 social events in a week where you marked "Focus" as the priority.
*   *SensAI Action:* Push Notification – *"Intervention Alert: You are violating your Sprint Goal. Recommend declining 'Dinner with Bob'."*

# 4. THE COMMAND CENTER: From Chaos to Structure

The Command Center is your "God Mode" interface. It is the single ingestion point for all reality.

### "Brain Dump" Technology
Stop categorizing effectively. Just dump.
*   **Voice Input:** *"SensAI, record this: Need to pay the electric bill on Tuesday, buy flowers for Sarah's anniversary, and remind me that I need to start training for the 5K."*

### The AI Synthesis Pipeline
1.  **Transcription:** Voice becomes text.
2.  **Intent Classification (Claude Sonnet 3.5):** The AI parses the stream.
3.  **Entity Extraction:**
    *   "Pay Electric Bill" -> **Task** (Context: Finance, Date: Next Tuesday).
    *   "Flowers for Sarah" -> **Task** (Context: Relationships, Tag: High Priority).
    *   "Train for 5K" -> **Challenge** (Context: Health).
4.  **Confidence Scoring:**
    *   *High Confidence:* Auto-created.
    *   *Low Confidence:* "Is 'Train for 5K' a single task or a new Project Epic?"

### The Draft Approval UI
Before polluting your clean database, AI creations sit in **Pending Drafts**.
*   You act as the Executive.
*   *Approve*, *Edit*, or *Reject* with a swipe.
*   This ensures your LifeOS remains a **High-Fidelity Source of Truth**.

# 7. ARCHITECTURE: Enterprise-Grade Engineering

Kaiz is built to scale to millions of high-performance users. This is not a "weekend app." It is a bank-grade platform.

### The Stack
*   **Backend Core:** **Java 21 (LTS)** running **Spring Boot 3.4.1**.
    *   *Why?* Proven stability. STRICT typing. Massive concurrency.
*   **Architecture:** **Hexagonal (Ports & Adapters)**.
    *   *Why?* The business logic is isolated. We can swap databases or APIs without breaking the "Agile Rules."
*   **Database:** **PostgreSQL 16** managed via **Flyway Migrations (v37)**.
    *   *Why?* Your life data is too precious for schema-less distinct chaos. We use strict relational integrity.
*   **AI Orchestration:** **Spring AI** interfacing with **Anthropic Claude 3.5 Sonnet**.
    *   *Why?* Claude provides the highest reasoning capability for complex intent extraction.

### Performance Engineering
*   **Mobile Client:** **Expo SDK 54 (React Native)** + **TypeScript**.
*   **FlashList Technology:** Renders lists of 5,000+ tasks at **60 FPS**.
*   **Optimistic UI:** Interactions feel instant (local-first), syncing in the background.
*   **Caffeine Caching:** 3-Tier intelligent caching strategy ensures static content loads in **<5 milliseconds**.

### Security Fortress
*   **JWT Architecture:** 15-minute short-lived tokens.
*   **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC):** Military-grade permission separation.
*   **API Rate Limiting (Bucket4j):** Protects the infrastructure from abuse giving premium users dedicated bandwidth.
