## üöÄ Getting Started

### Onboarding Experience
When users first open the app, they go through a personalized onboarding flow:

1. **Welcome Screen**
   - Enter first name and last name
   - Select how you heard about Kaiz (marketing insights)
   - Options: App Store, Social Media, Friend Referral, Podcast, Blog, Other

2. **Plan Type Selection**
   - **Individual** - Personal use for one person
   - **Family** - Shared workspace for families (premium feature)
   - **Corporate** - Enterprise deployment with company code

3. **Life Wheel Assessment**
   - Rate satisfaction in 9 life areas (1-10 scale)
   - Helps identify neglected areas that need attention
   - Categories: Health, Career, Finance, Personal Growth, Family, Social, Fun, Environment, Romance

4. **Goal Category Selection**
   - Choose focus areas from pre-built categories
   - Quick start bundles available for common life situations
   - Options include: Health & Fitness, Career Growth, Financial Freedom, Learning, Relationships

5. **Task Template Selection**
   - Pick from engaging pre-made task and epic templates
   - Templates include story points and recommended schedules
   - Builds your initial backlog for first 2-3 sprints

6. **Important Dates (Optional)**
   - Add birthdays, anniversaries, and special dates
   - Set reminder days before each date
   - Relationships: Family, Friend, Colleague, Other

7. **Account Creation**
   - Create account with email and password
   - Get a pre-populated sprint backlog to start immediately

## üìä Core Modules

### 1. Sprint Calendar (Main Dashboard)

The heart of the app - a weekly sprint-based calendar view where all life management happens.

**Features:**
- **Week View** - See all 7 days of the current sprint at a glance
- **Day View** - Detailed 24-hour schedule view like Outlook
- **Sprint Navigation** - Swipe to move between past, current, and future sprints
- **Sprint Naming** - Automatic naming like "S01-Jan-2026" (Sprint 1, January 2026)
- **Month Calendar Picker** - Expandable calendar to jump to any week/month

**Task Display Modes:**
1. **Eisenhower View** - Tasks grouped by urgency/importance quadrant:
   - Q1: Urgent + Important (üî• Do First)
   - Q2: Not Urgent + Important (üéØ Schedule - Growth Zone)
   - Q3: Urgent + Not Important (‚ö° Delegate/Quick)
   - Q4: Not Urgent + Not Important (üóëÔ∏è Eliminate)

2. **Status View** - Tasks grouped by:
   - To Do
   - In Progress
   - Done

3. **Size View** - Tasks grouped by story points:
   - Small (1-3 points)
   - Medium (5-8 points)
   - Large (13+ points)

**Task Cards Show:**
- Task title and description
- Story points badge
- Life wheel area with color coding
- Epic association (if any)
- Recurring task indicator
- Status indicator

### 5. SensAI - AI Scrum Master

SensAI is the intelligent AI coach that monitors your life execution and provides personalized guidance.

**Dashboard Features:**
- Coach messages with personalized insights
- Sprint health overview
- Today's standup status
- Active interventions requiring attention
- Quick action buttons

**Quick Actions:**
- Start Daily Standup
- Sprint Planning
- Quick Intake
- View Interventions
- Life Wheel Analysis

#### Daily Standup
A guided check-in ceremony:
1. **Yesterday Review** - See completed tasks, celebrate wins
2. **Today's Focus** - View and confirm today's priorities
3. **Blockers** - Report any obstacles
4. **Mood Check** - Log how you're feeling (Great, Good, Okay, Struggling)

#### Sprint Planning
Capacity-based planning ceremony:
1. **Set Capacity** - Based on velocity, set weekly point commitment
2. **Select Tasks** - Choose from backlog within capacity
3. **Review Distribution** - Ensure life wheel balance
4. **Commit** - Lock in sprint scope

#### Life Wheel Analysis
Visual representation of life balance:
- Radar chart showing all 9 dimensions
- Balance score (0-100)
- Trend indicators for each area
- Neglected area warnings
- Recovery task suggestions

**Example:** "Your Health dimension has been at 0% for 3 sprints. Suggested recovery: Add a 'Morning Walk' task (2 points) to balance your week."

#### Interventions System
SensAI proactively alerts you about issues:

**Intervention Types:**
- **Alert** (üö®) - Immediate issue blocking progress
- **Warning** (‚ö†Ô∏è) - Potential problem forming
- **Nudge** (üí°) - Suggestion or opportunity
- **Celebration** (üéâ) - Achievement recognition

**Intervention Categories:**
- **Capacity** - Overcommitment risks
- **Balance** - Life wheel neglect
- **Execution** - Sprint progress issues
- **Motivation** - Energy and engagement

**Example Interventions:**
- "‚ö†Ô∏è Overcommit Risk: You've planned 55 points but your average velocity is 28. Remove 27 points to ship reliably."
- "üí° Q2 Neglect: Your growth quadrant has been empty for 3 weeks. Add one strategic task to invest in your future."
- "üö® Sprint At Risk: Day 5 of 7, only 40% complete. Focus on high-value tasks or reduce scope."

#### Quick Intake
Universal capture system with AI processing:
- Text input for quick thoughts
- Voice recording with transcription
- Image/screenshot capture
- Camera for documents

**Intake Types:**
- Task
- Idea
- Commitment
- Goal

AI automatically:
- Extracts actionable items
- Suggests Life Wheel category
- Estimates story points
- Identifies optimal scheduling
- Detects conflicts with existing tasks

#### Velocity Tracking
Performance analytics:
- Current velocity (average of last 4 sprints)
- Velocity trend (up/down/stable)
- Committed vs. completed comparison
- Personal best tracking
- Projected capacity for next sprint

#### Sprint Retrospective
AI-guided reflection:
1. Check-in: How are you feeling?
2. What went well this sprint?
3. What didn't go as planned?
4. One improvement idea for next sprint

AI summarizes and creates action items.
